Greta Thunburg

The young environmentalist activist

Greta Tintin Eleonora Ernman Thunberg is a Swedish environmental activist who is known for challenging world leaders to take immediate action for climate change mitigation.

Gretas Top Accomplishments:

  1. Andrew Tate takedown
  2. Sailing to Madrid for COP25
  3. Strike at the Rikksdag
  4. Founding Fridays for Future
  5. Being nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize multiple times
